Agriculture is one of the biggest drivers of deforestation, and traceability is now essential to prove that commodities are produced responsibly. JANI AI helps exporters, cooperatives, and sourcing teams build farm-level traceability systems that support deforestation-free agriculture and stronger EUDR readiness.

## Why This Matters

Across agricultural supply chains, weak first-mile data makes it difficult to prove where products come from, how they were produced, and whether they are linked to deforestation. As regulations tighten, companies need more than paperwork—they need trusted, traceable farm data.

## What the EUDR Requires

The EU Deforestation Regulation (EUDR) requires operators and traders to show that relevant products are:

- **Deforestation-free**
- **Produced in line with the laws of the country of origin**
- **Supported by a due diligence statement**

This means agricultural supply chains need reliable traceability back to the plot of land.

## How JANI Supports EUDR

JANI captures the first-mile data needed for agricultural traceability and deforestation due diligence:

- Verified farmer profiles
- Field mapping and geolocation
- Plot boundaries and production records
- Farm activity and input logs
- Produce-to-lot linkage at collection
- Traceability reports and evidence packs
